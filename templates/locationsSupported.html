Supported Locations

Below are all the locations Bonnie can answer about the number of cases in the area. A quick way to find your area is to press and hold Control then press f. This will bring up a small input box on your screen which you can type your area info. If your area is supported it should take you to it's location on the page. We're very sorry that not all locations are available yet.
