How To Use Bonnie Bot

Bonnie Bot is a chatbot which was designed to answer questions specifically about COVID-19. We are continually updating this Website and we know that it can be so much better but we rely on your feedback to improve it! So please after having a go at this website type some feedback on the give feedback page!

What You Can Ask Her

Bonnie can answer a range of questions about Coronavirus such as what the most common symptoms are, what shielding is, and how to get support. Bonnie can also provide you with the latest number of cases in most UK areas and countries across the world. For a full list of the areas she can provided cases for, you can go to our page called "Locations" which can be found in the top menu bar.

Extra Features

Bonnie can read out her messages so that you don't have to read them yourself. To do this, click on the button in the bottom left corner. As we are continuously trying to improve Bonnie, we have included a feedback page where you can help us make her better. We have also included a tab with links to useful coronavirus websites.
